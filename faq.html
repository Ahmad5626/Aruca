FAQ's

  • Who can become a partner with Aruca Finance?

    Aruca Finance welcomes a wide range of professionals to become partners, including loan agents, ex-bankers, financial analysts, insurance agents, mutual fund agents, chartered accountants, builders, and professionals from various other fields. If you're interested in offering a diverse range of financial products and earning attractive commissions, we'd love to have you join us!

  • How to Become a DSA Partner With Aruca Finance?

    Becoming a Direct Selling Agent (DSA) partner with Aruca Finance is easy. Just follow these simple steps:

    • Step 1: Visit the Aruca Finance website and click on the 'Become a Partner' option.
    • Step 2: Fill in your details on the registration form and submit it.
    • Step 3: A member of our support team will reach out to guide you through the next steps and arrange a meeting.
    • Step 4: During the meeting, an Aruca Finance Manager will explain the lead generation process and provide you with all the necessary information.
    • Step 5: Once you're comfortable with the process, we’ll finalize everything and you'll sign an agreement with Aruca Finance.

    Once the agreement is signed and stamped, you’ll officially become an Aruca Finance DSA Agent. Join us today and begin your journey as a DSA Agent. Unlock the opportunity to earn lucrative commissions while assisting customers in securing the best financial products!

  • What are the Eligibility Criteria for Becoming a DSA Agent with Aruca Finance?

    To qualify as a DSA partner with Aruca Finance, you must meet the following criteria:

    • Age Requirement: You must be at least 25 years old.
    • Nationality: You must be a Resident Citizen of India.
    • Educational Qualifications: No specific educational qualifications are necessary.
    • Professional Background: Whether you're employed or running your own business, you can become an Aruca Finance loan agent. Ideal candidates include loan agents, ex-bankers, financial analysts, mutual fund agents, chartered accountants, and builders.

    Aruca Finance welcomes individuals from various professional backgrounds who are eager to broaden their income streams.

  • What Documents Are Required for DSA Registration with Aruca Finance?

    The following documents are required to complete your DSA registration with Aruca Finance:

    • Aadhaar Card
    • PAN Card
    • Two Passport-size Photographs
    • GST Registration (if applicable, for companies)
    • Proof of Employment: Salary slips (if employed), account statements and address proof (if running a business), or invoices and financial statements (if self-employed).
    • Bank Account Details: Your bank statement from the last 3 months.

    These documents are essential for KYC verification and will ensure a smooth and efficient registration process.

  • What Support Does Aruca Finance Provide to its DSAs?

    Aruca Finance is committed to supporting its DSAs through the following services:

    • Comprehensive Training: We offer in-depth training sessions to help you fully understand our products, processes, and the financial market.
    • Marketing Support: You’ll receive leads, marketing materials, and promotional strategies to help you successfully market our services.
    • Timely Payouts: We ensure that commissions are paid on time, with a transparent and efficient payout process.

    By partnering with Aruca Finance, you’ll receive all the tools and support you need to grow and succeed as a DSA.
